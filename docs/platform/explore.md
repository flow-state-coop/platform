# Explore

The **Explore** page at [`flowstate.network/explore`](https://flowstate.network/explore) is the front door to streaming funding on Flow State. It surfaces campaigns running across the platform's surfaces so you can find something to fund—or get inspiration for launching your own.

> Participate in Flow State streaming funding campaigns or launch your own.

## What you'll see

Campaigns are grouped into two sections:

- **Active** — campaigns currently streaming.
- **Completed** — campaigns that have concluded.

Each campaign is shown as a card with a name, logo, the surface it runs on, and live stats:

- **Total distributed** — the amount streamed to recipients so far, in the campaign's token (for example `ETHx`, `USDCx`, `G$`, or `USDN`).
- **Flow rate** — how fast the campaign is currently streaming.
- **Active donors** — the number of contributors with an open stream into the campaign.

The cards span the different building blocks Flow State offers and integrates with—**Flow Councils**, **Flow Guilds**, and integrations such as **Octant**, **Flow Caster**, and **GoodDollar**—so a single page gives you a cross-section of what's live. Selecting a card takes you to that campaign (an in-app page like a [Flow Guild](flow-guilds.md), or an external destination such as a partner app or Farcaster Mini App).

## Featured banner

At the top of the listing, the **Flow State Markee** banner highlights a featured message. It links out to the Markee, where anyone can pay to change the message that everyone visiting Explore sees.

:::info[Listed vs. unlisted]
Across Flow State, a [Flow Council](flow-councils/index.md) round or [Flow Splitter](flow-splitters/index.md) is either **Listed** or **Unlisted**. Only Listed campaigns are meant for public discovery surfaces; Unlisted ones stay fully functional and remain accessible to anyone with a **direct link**. New rounds and splitters default to Unlisted, so listing is opt-in.
:::

:::tip[Launch your own]
Ready to start a campaign? Spin up a [Flow Council](flow-councils/index.md) or a [Flow Splitter](flow-splitters/index.md) with the no-code launchpads.
:::
