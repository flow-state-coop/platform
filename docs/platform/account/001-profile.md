# Profile

Your **profile** is the public identity attached to your wallet address. Manage it at [`flowstate.network/profile`](https://flowstate.network/profile) — connect your wallet and **Sign In With Ethereum** to edit.

## Public information

This section is shown on your public profile:

- **Display Name** — letters, numbers, spaces, hyphens, and underscores, up to 50 characters.
- **Bio / Role** — a short description, up to 300 characters.
- **Social links** — **Twitter / X**, **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Farcaster**. Enter a handle or a full URL for each.

## Contact information

Your **Email** and **Telegram** are **private**. They're never shown on your public profile and are only shared when you choose to include them in an application — they auto-fill into application fields that ask for an email or Telegram.

Adding an email also enables [email notifications](003-notifications.md); you'll be asked to consent before Flow State sends you any.

Click **Save** to apply your changes.

## Public profiles

Every address has a public profile at `flowstate.network/profile/<address>`. It shows the display name (or a shortened address), bio, and any social links you've set, plus a **View projects** link to the [projects](/projects) that address manages. An address that hasn't set up a profile yet simply shows that no profile exists.
