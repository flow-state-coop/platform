# Flow Guilds

A **Flow Guild** is a lightweight, public funding page for a community or guild. Each guild lives at its own route (for example [`flowstate.network/flow-guilds/core`](https://flowstate.network/flow-guilds/core)) and gives supporters a single place to stream money to the guild and watch its funding live.

## What a guild page shows

- **Project details** — the guild's logo, name, description, and links (website, GitHub, X, Farcaster, Telegram, Discord), plus a copy-link button to share the page.
- **A live graph** — the guild's incoming flow, total donors, and, where the guild splits funds onward, its underlying [distribution pool](https://docs.superfluid.finance/docs/concepts/overview/distributions): members, their **units** (shares), and whether each is **connected**.
- **An activity feed** — recent streams, transfers, and distribution/share changes, with addresses resolved to ENS names where available.

Stats update in real time, and you can pick which **Super Token** to view from the guild's supported tokens.

## Funding a guild

Two ways to contribute, both from the guild page:

- **Open Flow** — start a continuous stream to the guild. Connect your wallet (and switch to the right network if prompted), then choose your token and rate.
- **Donate Once** — send a one-off contribution instead of an ongoing stream.

If you're a recipient in the guild's [Flow Splitter](flow-splitters/index.md) but haven't connected your shares, the page prompts you to connect so your [Super Token balance](https://app.superfluid.finance/) reflects in real time.

:::info[Guilds are curated]
Flow Guild pages are set up in coordination with Flow State. If you'd like a guild page for your community, [reach out on Telegram](https://t.me/+U6ZFRmOnFWo1ZGUx).
:::
