# Participating

How to take part in a Flow Council round, whatever your role:

- **[Applying](001-applying.md)** — create a project and submit it to a round's application form.
- **[Voting](002-voting.md)** — cast and recast your ballot as a Council Member, and claim votes on councils gated by an NFT.
- **[Receiving Funding](003-receiving-funding.md)** — connect to the distribution pool and watch funding arrive in real time.
- **[Grow the Pie](004-grow-the-pie.md)** — permissionlessly stream additional funding into any round.

Running a round yourself? See [Running a Round](../operators/index.md).
